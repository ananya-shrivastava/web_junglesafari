SAFARIS

Nandanvan Zoo and Safari, the area extends to 800 acres of land. Wild animals will be seen moving freely in their
respective zones where visitors can watch them through solar vehicles.Zones have been divided into herbivore safari,
tiger safari, bear safari and lion safari along with zoo for other animals.
Herbivore zone will include animals like cheetal, black-buck and sambar deer among 50 animals across an area of 30 hectares.
Bear zone will have four bears on 20 hectare, tiger zone will have three tigers on area of 20 hectares and lion safari will have 6-8 lions.
About Rs 684.29 crore has been spent on the project till December 2014 while estimated cost of project is around Rs 226 crore.
About 10,000 people can be accommodated in jungle safari at a time.

Initially, the animals would be kept in a small enclosure (soft release area and crawl) so that they could adaptto the environment following
which these animals will be released to their respective sectors sprawling in several acres,” he added.

Notably, the jungle safari will be comprised of four separate Safaris including Herbivore Safari,Bear Safari, Tiger and Lion Safari.
Besides, a zoo in 50 acres area carrying as many as 53 species will also be developed here.

As many as six Nilgais (two male and four female), five sambars (two male and three female) and 15spotted deer (five male and ten female) will
be shifted to ‘Herbivore Safari’ inside Jungle Safari spread in 30 hectares of land whereas the Tiger Safari had been developed in 20 hectares
area is going to house two tigers (a male and female).

Zones

The Whole 202.87 Ha area is planned into 8 Zone

  1. Monument Zone
  2. Parking Zone

    3. Parking space for more than 100 Buses, 300 Cars and 640 Two wheelers along with visitor’s and Driver’s Facilities.

  3. Administrative Zone

    4. Office and Residential Spaces for the official appointed for the Management

  4. Water Zone

    5. Special Features Like Floating Garden, Water Birds Area, Pathways

  5. Waiting Zone

    6. This Zone is especially for entertainment purpose of visitors as it has the features like Amphi-theatre, I-Max Theatre, Canteens,Food Courts,Museum, Orientation Center,Floating Deck,Nature Camp

  6. Zoo Zone

    7. The Zoo Zone has many Animal Enclosure with the Landscape theme of Indian Biographical Zone.

  7. Safari Zone
    8. This Zone has 4 Safari i.e.
    • Herbivore Safari
    • Bear Safari
    • Tiger Safari
    • Lion Safari
  8. Management Zone

    9. The No-Visitors Area having Veterinary Hospital, Rescue Center,Conservation & Breeding center and more.

NEW INTIATIVES

  • Around 166 personnel including officials, doctors, zoo-keeper, guard and others will be pressed into the task of taking care of animals, safari
    and also assist visitors in Nandanvan Zoo and Jungle Safari.
  • Due to some land related issue, the work of Lion Safari is pending, added the officer.
  • After accomplishment of some fencing related tasks, four sloth bears (two male and female) will be housed in Bear Safari being developed
    in area measuring around 20 hectare. Similarly, four bears will be kept in the zoo.
  • On completion of pending structures, works and approval of a few proposals by Central Zoo Authority (CZA) New Delhi,
